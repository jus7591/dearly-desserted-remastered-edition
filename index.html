Welcome! My name is Kelli Turner and I am a boutique baker. I would love to help you out with any kind of special event you may have, birthdays, anniversaries, wedding or baby showers, company events, church functions, holiday parties, or to simply say thank you.

I specialize in delicious dessert bars and cheesecake for any occasion. Dessert bars range from $1.25-$1.75 per bar depending on what type of bar. Plain cheesecake is $25 Cheesecake with add ins (Oreos, toffee bits, cherry) are $30
